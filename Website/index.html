Hoparoo Learning System

GOALS

Our goals for this project are to design and build an interactive learning mat we call Hoparoo! The final design guidelines are still not set in stone, but as we continue the development the finished prototype will be a set of working mats with a central receiver which a teacher or instructor can access and display results from the class. As of January 2016, we have a set of deliverables and a tentative timeline for the work progression. We hope to complete a working prototype by E-days so we can demonstrate the functionality.

MARKETING INTEGRATION

A first for ECE senior design, the Colorado State School of Business has joined forces with the ECE department to have independent study students integrate with senior design students. With this collaborative project, we hope to gain more market knowledge and learn the process of bringing an idea from design and engineering into a consumer product.
